## Structural phrases -> rewrite

| AI phrase | Human alternative |
|---|---|
| not just X, but also Y | Pick one or state it directly. |
| not X, but Y | Direct statement. |
| It's not about X, it's about Y | Direct statement. |
| Despite its X, Y faces challenges | State the challenges directly. |
| With its strategic X, Y continues to thrive | Delete; state what Y actually does. |
| from X to Y (false range) | Use "and" or pick one. |
| including but not limited to | including |
| such as (before exhaustive list) | including |

## Punctuation

| AI habit | Fix |
|---|---|
| Em dash — like this — | Comma, colon, or split sentence. |
| Three-item list ("fast, reliable, and scalable") | Pick the strongest one. |
| Bolding 3+ words per paragraph | Strip extras. |
| Title Case Headings | Sentence case. |
| Emoji in headings | Remove. |

## Apply order

1. Pass 1: mechanical find/replace on words (verbs, nouns, adjectives, openers).
2. Pass 2: pattern scan (em dashes, trailing -ing, rule of three, negative parallelism).
3. Pass 3: read aloud. Anything that sounds like a brochure, rewrite.
4. Pass 4: confirm meaning, length (±15%), proper nouns, and numbers are intact.
