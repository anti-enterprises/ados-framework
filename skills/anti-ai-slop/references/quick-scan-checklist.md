# Quick Scan Checklist

Fast in-flight scan for any copy under 300 words. Run top to bottom.

## 1. Word scan (case-insensitive)

If any of these appear, mark and replace:

```
Additionally (as opener)    align with             crucial
delve                        emphasizing            enduring
enhance                      fostering              garner
highlight (as verb)          interplay              intricate
intricacies                  key (as adjective)     landscape (abstract)
pivotal                      showcase               tapestry
testament                    underscore             valuable
vibrant                      serves as              stands as
seamlessly                   leverage (verb)        empower
comprehensive                holistic               robust
cutting-edge                 game-changer           It is worth noting
rich tapestry                evolving landscape     dive into
in today's [X] world         at the end of the day  navigate the
unlock                       unleash                supercharge
elevate                      transform your         revolutionize
foster                       streamline             optimize (vague)
```

## 2. Pattern scan

Look for:
- **Em dashes** ( — ) used in place of commas/colons. Replace.
- **Three-item lists** in a single sentence ("fast, reliable, and scalable"). Cut to one.
- **"Not just X, but Y"** or **"Not X, but Y"**. Rewrite as direct statement.
- **"Despite its X, Y faces challenges"** formula. Cut the formula, state the challenge plainly.
- **Title Case Headings** like "Strategic Negotiations And Partnerships". Convert to sentence case.
- **Inline-header bullets**: `• **Label:** description`. Either bold label alone or use a clean table.
- **Emoji in headings** (🧠 🚀 ✨ etc.). Remove unless explicitly requested.
- **"Let's explore" / "Here is your" / "As we can see" / "It is important to note"**. Cut entirely.
- **Knowledge-cutoff hedges** ("as of my last training update"). Cut entirely.
- **"is/are/has" inflated**: "serves as / stands as / represents / marks / features / boasts". Replace with the plain verb.
- **Bolding 3+ words per paragraph**. Strip extras, keep one if genuinely needed.
- **Synonym chains**: the same noun called 4 different things in 4 sentences. Repeat the noun.
- **"From X to Y" false ranges**: when X and Y don't form a real scale. Rewrite.

## 3. Sentence-shape scan

- Any sentence that ends with `, [present participle] ...` (e.g., "...creating a lively community", "...ensuring scalability", "...fostering growth") — cut the participle clause. The fact alone is enough.
- Any sentence with a "this allows us to / this enables / this ensures" tail — cut the tail.
- Any sentence longer than 30 words with no concrete noun — rewrite or split.

## 4. Structure scan (for full pages/articles)

- Sections named "Future Outlook", "Challenges and Legacy", "Conclusion", "In Summary" — likely AI formula. Rewrite or remove.
- A "See Also" / "Related" section filled with broad parent categories instead of actually related items — rewrite.
- Sudden style or quality shifts within the same piece — flag as mixed human/AI.

## 5. Verdict

After the scan, give one of:
- **Clean** — 0 hits, ready to ship
- **Minor** — 1-3 hits, easy fix
- **Heavy slop** — 4+ hits or any forbidden word in the headline/hero

Heavy slop requires a full rewrite, not surface edits.
